Paris

Paris is the capital city of France and one of the most renowned cities in the world. Known as the "City of Light," Paris is famous for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture.

The city is home to iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Louvre Museum. Paris is also celebrated for its charming cafes, historic streets, and romantic atmosphere.
